CRATE CHAOS

(TDM Game Map)

I've  crafted a game map within Unreal Engine, drawing inspiration from iconic titles like Call of Duty and other thrilling action shooters. Through my creative prowess as an Unreal Engine artist and game designer, I've meticulously designed this entire game level from the ground up This map is essentially fashioned as a firearm arena, providing an engaging environment for players.

Conceptualization
I have  formulated an entire map through AI-driven designs. My process commenced with the collection of various references, which served as wellsprings of inspiration. Subsequently, I translated my ideas into concrete form by crafting 2D conceptual levels on physical sheets of paper.
Assets

I create intricate 3D model assets and apply textures to them using Substance Painter. I leverage the Nanite system to optimize these assets for seamless performance in the Unreal Engine, eliminating the need to produce lower-polygon models.

Level Design

Initially, I begin by sculpting a terrain landscape for the game level. Following that, I proceed to design the level layout by creating the necessary barriers and pathways on the map. Subsequently, I import various assets into the project, including textures to enhance the visual appeal. Finally, I place these assets onto the terrain to bring the game environment to life.
I utilized a fundamental system to scatter foliage by hand, aiming for a balanced approach without excessive layering. I endeavored to replicate a consistent foliage arrangement. Lighting was established using Unreal Engine's inherent basic system. In the final build, I opted for low static shadows to complement the foliage's aesthetic.

ANSH SINGLA

I am Ansh Singla, a passionate 3D modeler and game artist. Welcome to my creative corner! I'm thrilled to share my journey as a 3D artist with you. From a young age, I've been captivated by the magic of turning imagination into tangible art.
