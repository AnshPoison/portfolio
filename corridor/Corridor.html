Corridor

(VR Immersive Environment)

I have successfully executed a comprehensive project by leveraging the powerful capabilities of Unreal Engine, Blender, and Substance Painter. With a primary focus on immersive technologies such as Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR), my project showcases a meticulously crafted environment that is brought to life through a sophisticated AI Non-Player Character (NPC) system.

I have meticulously created captivating characters using the character creator tool, ensuring a high level of detail and realism. I've added integrating tangible objects for interactive picking within the virtual space. Additionally, I've implemented level trigger boxes that facilitate the transition into distinct game levels.

Assets

(3d Modelling & Texturing)

The environment is enriched with lifelike textures derived from meticulously chosen JPEG and PNG images, meticulously curated to enhance realism. Additionally, the implementation of normal maps or height maps further augments the visual aesthetics

This project was developed prior to the introduction of the nanite system in Unreal Engine. As a result, we had to design low-polygon models for the scene and utilize low-value textures. This approach was necessary to ensure compatibility with low-end graphic cards, as transparent and reflective textures posed challenges in terms of performance. However, we were able to leverage the capabilities of Unreal Engine to effectively implement lighting and textures within the project.
