RENDER

The term "render" refers to the process of creating a final image or animation from a 3D scene. It's the step where I bring my virtual models to life and make them look realistic. In Blender, I can create amazing 3D models with materials, textures, lighting setups, and different camera angles. But to see the final result, I render the scene.

The final process is post processing. Post-processing is like adding the finishing touches to a masterpiece, ensuring that the final result matches the creator's intent and vision.

Evee
(Fx Simultions)
My goal is to optimize the simulations for smooth rendering on lower-end PCs. Additionally, I employ Ember Gen, a fantastic software, to generate various smoke simulations effortlessly. This software particularly excels in creating realistic smoke and fire effects.
I employ sophisticated FX simulations to add a touch of realism and dynamism, further elevating the impact of my renders.When it comes to creating visual effects simulations in Blender, I have a good understanding of the process.
Passes
I initiate scene creation by establishing the layout ("blocking"). Next, I craft 3D models for assets and characters. Lighting is then added for ambiance and shadows. Further, I apply detailed textures and colors for realism. Depth is introduced using 3D techniques. Finally, rendering produces the end result.

ANSH SINGLA

I am Ansh Singla, a passionate 3D modeler and game artist. Welcome to my creative corner! I'm thrilled to share my journey as a 3D artist with you. From a young age, I've been captivated by the magic of turning imagination into tangible art.
